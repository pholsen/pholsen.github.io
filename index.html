Game Designer and Unity Developer

'Folk's is a city and also a kingdom. There is a sea beside Folk city called "Aysar". Folk's citizens are afraid of water and their religion says that they shouldn't touch the big waters. One day a man called "The Explorer" comes to Folk's and says to everyone that "There is a land across the Aysar". The story begins.'

What I worked on:

  • Led design and development, establishing core gameplay.
  • Wrote narrative and dialogue.

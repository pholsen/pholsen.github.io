About Me

Hi! I'm a passionate Game Designer and Developer currently based in Warsaw. With a Bachelor's in Digital Game Design and ongoing Master's studies in New Media, I specialize in creating engaging mechanics and narrative-driven experiences.

My journey includes hands-on experience as a Game Design Intern at Root-Istanbul and a Game Tester at Digi Game Startup Studio. I love blending creativity with technical skills in Unity to build games that not only function well but also tell compelling stories. Whether it's designing card mechanics for deck-builders or debugging complex levels, I'm always ready for the next challenge.

Folk’s Quest

Game Designer and Unity Developer

A 2D rouge-like deck building card game.

“The Folk” is a city and also a kingdom. There is a sea beside Folk city called “Asvar” Folk’s citizens are afraid of water and their religion says that they shouldn't touch the big waters. One day A man called “The Explorer” comes to Folk and says to everyone that “There is a land across the Asvar”. The story begins.

What I worked on:
  • Led design and development, establishing core gameplay.
  • Designing cards, relics, potions and all other stuff
  • Wrote narrative and dialogue.
  • Prototyping in Unity Engine
